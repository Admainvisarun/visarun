NTRODUCTION


Welcome to sherpa’s Privacy Policy (the Policy).

This version of the Policy was last updated on August 02, 2023.

1. Overview:

Visarunn respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the privacy of the users our eVisa application platform. This privacy policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use and protect it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

2. Information We Collect:
a. Personal Information: During the eVisa application process, we may collect personal information such as your name, contact details, passport information, and other relevant data required for visa processing.
b. Device and Usage Information: We may collect information about your device, browser, IP address, and other technical data when you use our platform.

3. Use of Information:
a. Visa Processing: We collect your personal information to process and submit your eVisa application on your behalf to the relevant immigration authorities.
b. Communication: We may use your contact information to communicate with you regarding the status of your eVisa application, provide updates, and respond to your inquiries.
c. Improving Our Services: We may analyze usage patterns and feedback to enhance our platform's functionality, user experience, and customer service.

4. Data Security:
We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Data Retention:
We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy and as required by applicable laws or regulations. Once your eVisa application is processed and concluded, we will securely delete or anonymize your personal data.

6. Third-Party Disclosure:
We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties unless necessary for visa processing, as required by law, or with your consent. We may share your data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our platform, subject to strict confidentiality obligations.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies:
We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our platform. You have the option to disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may impact certain features and functionality.

8. Links to Third-Party Websites:
Our platform may contain links to external websites. We do not control or endorse the content, privacy practices, or security of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies before providing any personal information.

9. Children's Privacy:
The Visarunn platform is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child, we will promptly delete it.

10. Your Rights:
You have the right to request access, correction, erasure, or restriction of your personal information held by us. You also have the right to withdraw your consent or lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority.

11. Changes to the Privacy Policy:
We reserve the right to modify or update this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on our platform, and it is your responsibility to review and remain informed about any revisions.

12. Contact Us:
If you have any questions, concerns, or requests relating to this privacy policy, please contact us at Admin@joinvisarun.com.
