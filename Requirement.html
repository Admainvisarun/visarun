Check visa requirements for your nationality for any country in the world.

Check Visa Requirements of All Countries

Before planning to visit another country, all travelers should learn how to check visa requirements for their nationality in case they require a travel document to enter the country of destination.

Visarun provides a comprehensive service whereby travelers can check if they require a visa to travel to all countries in the world with a passport from their country, and learn about the steps to take to obtain the necessary travel document, if applicable.


Visa requirements vary by country and also depend on the nationality of the traveler, as well as the duration and purpose of the intended stay. You may be able to travel to your country of destination visa-free depending on the purpose of the stay, or it may be necessary to obtain a visa or an electronic travel authorization.

For short stays, many countries require certain nationalities to obtain a visa in advance of travel from an embassy or consulate, while others permit select citizens to obtain a visa online through an electronic application system for purposes of transit, business or tourist travel.

Travelers who wish to stay for significant periods of time in a country, or visit for purposes of working or studying, are normally required to apply for a consular visa from the nearest diplomatic office of their destination.

Find out what countries require a visa to visit from your country of nationality by browsing through the overview of visa requirements around the world below.

Select your nationality or the nationality you are interested in to find out more details about the visa requirements.

Visa Requirements by Nationality
The visa requirements for your nationality will vary depending on the country or territory you plan to visit, as most territories have their own individual visa policies with different requirements for different foreign citizens.

It may not be necessary to obtain a travel document to visit your country of destination, as many countries have a passport nationality list of visa-exempt citizens who they permit to enter visa-free for short periods of time.

However, it may be necessary to obtain either a visa or electronic travel visa authorization for your nationality to visit your desired destination.

If you do require a electronic visa for a specific country, an online visa application may be available for your nationality. Otherwise, it may be necessary to apply for an embassy visa in person from the nearest diplomatic office of the destination country.
